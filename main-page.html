Welcome to Honkai: Star Rail

About the game

Honkai: Star Rail is a free-to-play turn-based role-playing game developed by HoYoverse, the creators of Honkai Impact 3rd and Genshin Impact. Step aboard the Astral Express, a mysterious train traveling across the galaxy, and explore unique worlds full of secrets, challenges, and adventures. Each planet offers its own culture, environment, and mysteries: from the icy landscapes of Belobog, the advanced civilization of Xianzhou, the luxurious halls of Penacony, to the mysterious and ancient ruins of Amphoreus.

Form a team of heroes with distinct abilities and personalities, strategize in turn-based battles, and uncover hidden treasures along the way. With stunning visuals, immersive music, and ever-changing events, Honkai: Star Rail delivers a captivating adventure for players who love exploration, strategy, and rich storytelling.
