Welcome to Honkai: Star Rail

About the game

Honkai: Star Rail is a free-to-play turn-based role-playing game developed by HoYoverse, the creators of Honkai Impact 3rd and Genshin Impact. Step aboard the Astral Express, a mysterious train traveling across the galaxy, and explore unique worlds full of secrets, challenges, and adventures. Each planet offers its own culture, environment, and mysteries: from the icy landscapes of Belobog, the advanced civilization of Xianzhou, the luxurious halls of Penacony, to the mysterious and ancient ruins of Amphoreus.

Form a team of heroes with distinct abilities and personalities, strategize in turn-based battles, and uncover hidden treasures along the way. With stunning visuals, immersive music, and ever-changing events, Honkai: Star Rail delivers a captivating adventure for players who love exploration, strategy, and rich storytelling.

What Makes HSR Unique:

  • Astral Express - more than just transportation. It's your home, where you can interact with your crew, manage your team, and plan your next mission
  • Exploration of Diverse Worlds - each planet and city has its own culture, environment, and mysteries. From the icy landscapes of Belobog to the futuristic halls of Xianzhou, luxurious setting of Penacony, and Amphoreus, an isolated world with ancient ruins. There's always always something to discover
  • Turn-Based Combat System - strategize carefully in battles, combining heroes' abilities, elemental effects, and tactical positioning to overcome enemies
  • Unique Heroes and Crew - recruit heroes with distinct skills and personalities. Form your team wisely to adapt to each mission and encounter
  • Rich Visuals and Music - experience stunning artwork, animated sequences, and an immersive soundtrack that brings each world to life
  • Events and Updates - the game constantly introduces new story events, challenges, and rewards, keeping players engaged and excited
